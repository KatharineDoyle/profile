About Me

Hey! I'm Katharine, I completed fullstack web development bootcamp at Le Wagon in Berlin 2018, and am now a frontend and backend web developer!

I'm originally from London, and completed my studies in Sculpture and Art History in Edinburgh. I became interested in coding after building a responsive sculpture with Arduino sofware, and became aware of the benefits that learning to code would have for both my artistic practice and my career.

My Projects

ARTHUDDLE

Discover your creative potential.

ARTHUDDLE is a website application that aims to simplify the process of searching and booking art workshops and classes. Features included a 'drag-and-drop' search function, a personalised dashboard, and shared note-taking for each class.

Spirited Away

Create personalised cocktail lists.

A project completed during Le Wagon allowing users to create personalised lists of their favourite cocktails. Features included adding ingredients from a dropdown list and adding a new cocktail.

About Me

Hey! I'm Katharine, I completed fullstack web development bootcamp at Le Wagon in Berlin 2018, and am now a frontend and backend web developer!

I'm originally from London, and completed my studies in Sculpture and Art History in Edinburgh. I became interested in coding after building a responsive sculpture with Arduino sofware, and became aware of the benefits that learning to code would have for both my artistic practice and my career.

My Projects
